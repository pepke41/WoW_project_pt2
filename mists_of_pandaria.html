Mists of Pandaria

After the Shattering and the defeat of Deathwing the Horde and Alliance are more at odds than ever, and now both are fighting to conquer the uncharted land of Pandaria. Pandaria is home to the Pandaren. In this expansion players will be able to level to 90, quest in Pandaria, and play as one new class, and play as one new races

New Race

Players can now play as one of the natives of Pandaria. Pandaren are different than the other races as they can be both Horde and Alliance. Once the player has gone through all of the Pandaren starter quests they can choose which faction they would like to join. Pandaren are the only race that cannot be a death knight.

New Class

Players can now play as the monk class. The monk class can be played by most races (goblins and worgen cannot be monks). The monk class has three specializations Brewmaster (tank), Mistweaver (Heal), Windwalker (dps).
