World of Warcraft

World of Warcraft is MMORPG that was created in 2004. Over the years it has gone through many changes, has five expansions, and recently produced a movie. This page will give a brief introduction to the game, and what was available to a player in 2004. Each of the of the other tabs will focus on the following expansions. Also there is a page to vote and rank the WoW expansions. Enjoy!
